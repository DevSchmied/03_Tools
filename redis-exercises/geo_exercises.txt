2. Add the coordinates of the city Catania (latitude 37.502669, longitude 15.087269) 
and the city Messina (latitude 37.739831, longitude 14.907847) to the key cities.

GEOADD cities 37.502669 15.087269 "Catania" 37.739831 14.907847 "Messina"


3. Find the distance between Palermo and Catania in kilometers in the key cities.

GEODIST cities "Palermo" "Catania" km


4. Find all cities located within a radius of 100 km from the coordinates (longitude 15.087269, latitude 37.502669) in the key cities.

GEORADIUS cities 15.087269 37.502669 100 km


5. Find all cities located within a radius of 50 km from the city Catania in the key cities.

GEORADIUSBYMEMBER cities "Catania" 50 km
